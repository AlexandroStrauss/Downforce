Airplanes fly using the aerodynamic principle of LIFT.

Flip an airplane wing upside down, and you get the opposite effect...

DOWNFORCE

A game by Alexandro Strauss

INSTRUCTIONS

  • Drive with the arrow or WASD keys:
  • Up or W to accelerate,
  • down or S to brake,
  • left / right or A / D to steer.
  • If you crash, press R to reset.
The faster your car goes, the more pounds of downforce it generates.
More downforce = better handling.
  • Set the quickest lap time you can!
